# Next steps

[English](../en-US/11-next-steps.md) | [Português do Brasil](../pt-BR/11-next-steps.md)

Extend capability discovery when released Go APIs support cross-chain USDT/USDC; add quote expiry, minimum, slippage, conversion, and two-leg reconciliation. Explore a stable treasury balance, external signer, secure treasury policies, webhooks or a reconciliation worker, production authentication/authorization, recipient profiles with saved but re-confirmed payment preferences, QR scanning, and richer assignment/review workflows.

Keep each extension behind the application-facing `PaymentService`. Do not leak provider models or turn the navigation into a wallet product. See the focused notes: [multi-asset](12-multi-asset-payouts.md), [USDT/USDC](13-usdt-usdc.md), [conversion](14-asset-conversion.md), [stable balance](15-stable-balance.md), [external signer](16-external-signer.md), and [production treasury](17-production-treasury-security.md).
