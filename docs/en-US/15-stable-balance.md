# Stable balance

[English](../en-US/15-stable-balance.md) | [Português do Brasil](../pt-BR/15-stable-balance.md)

A stable-balance policy can reduce treasury volatility but introduces issuer, token, conversion, liquidity, and operational risks. Make activation explicit, validate token identity, and account for conversions separately from contributor payouts.
