# Code of conduct

[English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | [Português do Brasil](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.pt-BR.md)

We commit to a welcoming, harassment-free community regardless of background, identity, experience, or economic circumstance. Be respectful, assume good intent while addressing concrete harm, accept constructive feedback, protect participant privacy, and never pressure anyone to reveal wallet secrets or use real funds.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, financial coercion, and publishing private security reports. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Report conduct issues privately to repository maintainers. Enforcement should be fair, confidential, and proportionate.
